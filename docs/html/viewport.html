Mouse¶

Hold Left Mouse¶

  • Left and right to look around.

  • Forward and backwards to walk.

Hold Middle Button¶

Panning.

Hold right Mouse¶

  • Move mouse to look around

  • Use W, S, A, D to walk like a first person shooter game.

  • Use E and Q to move up and down respectively
